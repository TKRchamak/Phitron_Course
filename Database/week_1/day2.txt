ER - Entity Relationship

ERD use for Database Modeling

* Entity - student , teacher, result
* Attribute - Entity's data
* Relationship - Entity's Relationship

Relationship:
        -> Connectivity (table connection)
        -> Cardinality (1:1, 1:N, M:N == one to one, one to many, many to many)
        

============================================
Participation (ongsogrohon):
        -> Total Participation (akta Entity set er sobai jodi participate kore tahole seta total)
        -> Partial Participation (sobai jodi participate na kore)
